Weekly Words

AMBITION

Definition: A strong desire to achieve something.

Example: "She had ambition to become a pilot."

ANCIENT

Definition: Having the qualities of something very old.

Example: "The artifact belonged to ancient times."

ADVICE

Definition: A suggestion about what someone should do.

Example: "He needed advice from an expert."

APPROACH

Definition: A way of dealing with something.

Example: "They had to come up with a way to approach the situation."

ARRANGE

Definition: To put things in a neat, attractive, or required order.

Example: "He needed to arrange the books in alphabetical order."
